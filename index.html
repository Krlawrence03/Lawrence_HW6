ABOUT KYLE

I’m a senior at the University of Iowa majoring in Business Analytics and Information Systems. Originally from Eagan, Minnesota, I graduated from Eagan High School before coming to Iowa City.

I currently serve as the Interfraternity Council President and am a proud member of Phi Gamma Delta (Fiji). My experiences in leadership and student engagement have shaped my passion for bringing people together and driving data-informed decision-making.

Professionally, I’m passionate about data analysis and visualization. During my internship at Donaldson Company, Inc., I worked in Revenue Operations on a variety of data visualization and data management projects that supported the Mobile Solutions Sales division.

Outside of academics and work, I’m constantly looking for ways to apply data to real-world business challenges and strengthen my technical skills in analytics tools such as Power BI and SQL.
